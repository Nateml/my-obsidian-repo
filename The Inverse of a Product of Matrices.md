# The Inverse of a Product of Matrices
The inverse of a product of matrices can be found by [[Matrix Multiplication|multiplying]] the inverse of each matrix with each other in reverse order.

> $AB(B^{-1}A^{-1})=I$

Likewise (if square)

> $B^{-1}A^{-1}AB=I$
