# Span of Vectors
>The set of all possible vectors that can be reached by a linear combination of a given set of vectors.

The span of two vectors should fill their 2 dimensional [[Vector Space|vector space]] providing the two vectors are not [[Linearly Dependent Vectors|linearly dependent]].
